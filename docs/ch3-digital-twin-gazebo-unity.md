## Spec-Kit Plus Block

- **Learning Objectives**
  - Define a digital twin for a humanoid robot.
  - Configure Gazebo and Unity scenes linked to ROS 2.
  - Stream telemetry between simulation and control nodes.

- **Required Skills**
  - ROS 2 basics, 3D coordinate frames.

- **System Components**
  - Gazebo world, humanoid URDF/SDF.
  - Unity scene with ROS–TCP.

- **Inputs & Outputs**
  - Simulated sensor data.
  - Commanded joint trajectories.

- **Tools & Frameworks**
  - Gazebo, `gazebo_ros` packages.
  - Unity + ROS–TCP Connector.

- **Performance Constraints**
  - Real-time or near real-time simulation rate.

## Chapter Body

_This chapter will be expanded with full workflows for building, launching, and validating humanoid digital twins in Gazebo and Unity, with ROS 2 integration and Python/launch file examples._
