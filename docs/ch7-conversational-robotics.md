## Spec-Kit Plus Block

- **Learning Objectives**
  - Architect conversational pipelines for humanoid robots.
  - Integrate ASR, NLU, and TTS with ROS 2.
  - Ground conversational turns in robot state and environment.

- **Required Skills**
  - Python, basic NLP.

- **System Components**
  - Microphone array, speakers.
  - ASR, LLM backend, TTS.

- **Inputs & Outputs**
  - Inputs: speech waveforms.
  - Outputs: synthesized speech, robot actions.

- **Tools & Frameworks**
  - ASR/TTS APIs, ROS 2 bridges.

- **Performance Constraints**
  - Low-latency dialogue (target < 1.5 s turn-around).
