## Spec-Kit Plus Block

- **Learning Objectives**
  - Explain mechanical, electrical, and software co-design for humanoids.
  - Understand URDF/SRDF modeling and kinematic chains.
  - Plan and validate motions using ROS 2 and simulation.

- **Required Skills**
  - ROS 2, basic kinematics.

- **System Components**
  - Humanoid chassis, actuators, sensors.
  - Control PC and communication buses.

- **Inputs & Outputs**
  - Joint targets, trajectories.
  - State estimates, contact information.

- **Tools & Frameworks**
  - ROS 2, MoveIt, Gazebo.

- **Performance Constraints**
  - Stable walking and manipulation under disturbances.

## Chapter Body

_This chapter will be expanded with detailed humanoid modeling, control architecture, and motion planning pipelines, including ROS 2 and MoveIt examples._
