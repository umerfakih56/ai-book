- **Learning Objectives**

  - Basic networking and Linux administration.

- **System Components**

- **Inputs & Outputs**
  - Telemetry streams, logs.
  - Control interfaces, alerts.

- **Tools & Frameworks**
  - ROS 2, monitoring stacks (Prometheus, Grafana), CI/CD for robots.

- **Performance Constraints**
  - High availability and safe operation.
